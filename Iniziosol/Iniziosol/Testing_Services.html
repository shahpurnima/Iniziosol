      Testing Services

      Testing Services: Enterprises have realized the potential of QA testing functions as part of the software development life cycle (SDLC) to successfully manage business and technology priorities. Inadequate software testing increases business risk, reduces revenue due to system failure and downtime, leads to wastage of time and resources, higher application maintenance costs (due to poor quality software going into production) and stakeholder dissatisfaction. With business value becoming a key focus, organizations have started aligning testing and QA methodologies to support the shift from "completing IT projects" to "delivering optimized business outcomes". To leverage this shift organizations continuously endeavor to have an independent and dedicated testing function. these are times of rapidly changing technologies, industry dynamics, competitive pressure, regulatory compliance, and demanding business partners and customers.These pose new challenges for enterprises and necessitate the quick deployment of robust quality software applications that can enable the expansion of business channels, lower business costs, and ensure compliance. Functional testing for such unique requirements has become important for business assurance. Iniziosol Technologies offers functional testing to verify each function of the software application in conformance with the requirement specification. We perform black box testing to test all the functionalities of the system and compare actual results with the expected results.
